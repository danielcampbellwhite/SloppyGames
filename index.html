About Sloppy Games

Sloppy Games is a small family-run website where Marley and his dad make free browser games together. They came up with the ideas, the name, and that gloriously sloppy logo as a team.

Everything here will always be free to play — no logins, no ads pushed in your face, no nonsense. Just click and play. The goal is to build one little game at a time and get better as they go.

— The Sloppy Games team 💜💚
