About Sloppy Games

Sloppy Games is a small family-run website where Marley and his dad make free browser games together. They came up with the ideas, the name, and that gloriously sloppy logo as a team.

Everything here will always be free to play — no logins, no nonsense. Just click and play. The goal is to build one little game at a time and get better as they go.

About the ads: we run small ads on the site to help cover hosting so Sloppy Games can stay free forever. We promise never to use ads that interrupt your games, pop up over them, or block them behind a paywall. Hit play, have fun — that's the whole deal.

— The Sloppy Games team 💜💚
